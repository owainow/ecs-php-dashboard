About ECS

ECS allows for event organisers to create an event at their showground. Show jumpers can then view a list of created events and can pay and book in using our online booking system. We also offer a scheduling system so competitors are always kept up to date

Icon

Creating an Event

Our bespoke website can be used by event organisers to easily create events using a pre made form for ease of use.

Icon

Booking into Events

Our Bespoke website can be used by competitors to book into events near you, all you need to do is fill out a simple form.

Icon

Secure Payments

Use our website for fast and secure pre payments for your equestrian bookings. No need for cash on the day.

Icon

Event Scheduling

At no extra cost to the event organiser a scheduling operation can be performed so that all competitors have time slots for their rounds.

Icon

Distance From Venue

No need for stressful warm ups, we now schedule riders based on the distance they have to travel to the venue.

Icon

Live updates

You can view the time of your round from your phone any updates to the time of your round you will be notified via e-mail or text.

Competing made easier with ECS

At ECS it is all about keeping things simple. Making doing what we love much more efficient! Our goal is to enusre that event organisers have a much simpler way of creating events and scheduling events so that competitors can spend more time doing what they love. Competing!
